Baligyaan isn't just a snack — it's a pasalubong from someone who cares, a merienda that brings the family together, a taste that takes you straight back to the province. We make food the way your lola would — with patience, with real ingredients, and with love you can taste.

Our Story

Rooted in Aklanon Pride

Baligyaan was born from a simple belief: that the best snacks come from honest ingredients and time-honored recipes. From our kitchen in Kalibo, Aklan — the home of the Ati-Atihan festival — we craft every batch with the same fiesta spirit and family warmth that Aklanons are known for.

Using only fresh, locally sourced ingredients, we cook in small batches to ensure every piece meets our standard. No artificial flavors. No artificial colors. No preservatives — just the pure, vibrant taste of the Philippines in every bite.
